ShakeyMilk

Come on in to ShakeyMilk and experience the most delicious, freshest, hand-crafted milkshakes in town! Our classic diner-style shop whips up creamy, dreamy shakes made with only the finest ingredients - real ice cream, fresh milk, and your choice of mix-ins and toppings. Sip on a velvety vanilla, rich chocolate, fresh strawberry or choose from our ever-changing array of unique flavors. We serve up shakes, malts, floats and sodas in a fun, retro atmosphere perfect for enjoying a cold, indulgent treat with family or friends. At ShakeyMilk, every shake is hand-spun specially for you. Stop by today for an authentic old-fashioned milkshake experience!
